About LOGICA

Latin Organization for Growth in Computing and Academics

Our Mission: Increasing the participation and success of students from Latinx and underrepresented communities pursuing careers in the field of computing and computer science.

We are a network of students working together to develop technical, leadership, and professional skills through an environment that encourages camaraderie and growth not only as an individual, but as a community.

We are an organization dedicated to inclusivity and welcome all who share our vision.
